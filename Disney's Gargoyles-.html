About the Series

The Manhattan Clan is a group of gargoyles who have been living and working in the Manhattan neighborhood for generations. They have adapted to their environment, including the use of technology and the impact of warfare.

The series has been adapted into various media, including video games, films, and television shows. The Manhattan Clan has earned millions of dollars from the series' success and continues to produce new episodes.

Gargoyles Game

The Gargoyles Game is a text-based adventure game created by Erika Sanders. The player controls Goliath, Hudson, Brooklyn, Lexington, Broadway, Angela,Elisa Maza, and Bronx, and must survive in the Manhattan Clan's city. The game features a storyline that follows the lives of these characters and their allies.

Gargoyles: The Movie

Gargoyles is an animated series that aired from 1994 to 1997. It follows a clan of gargoyles who have been frozen in stone for a thousand years and are awakened in modern-day New York City. They must adapt to the modern world while protecting the city from various threats.
